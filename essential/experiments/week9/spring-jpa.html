DATASOURCE

The datasource should be configured in the spring datasourcebean

                       <bean id="dataSource" class="org.springframework.jdbc.datasource.DriverManagerDataSource">
                    <property name="username" value="admin***"/>
                    <property name="password" value="******"/>
                    <property name="url" value="jdbc:mysql://******/booksandmusicstack"/>
                    <property name="driverClassName" value="com.mysql.jdbc.Driver"/>
                    </bean>

HIBERNATE

I am going to use Hibernate for persistence in my project.

                        <bean id="entityManagerFactory" class="org.springframework.orm.jpa.LocalContainerEntityManagerFactoryBean">
                        <property name="dataSource" ref="dataSource"/>
                        <property name="jpaVendorAdapter">
                            <bean class="org.springframework.orm.jpa.vendor.HibernateJpaVendorAdapter"/>
                        </property>
                        <property name="jpaProperties">
                            <map>
                                <entry key="hibernate.hbm2ddl.auto" value="create-drop"/>
                                <entry key="hibernate.show_sql" value="true"/>
                            </map>
                        </property>
                     <property name="packagesToScan" value="edu.neu.project.model"/>
                     </bean>
